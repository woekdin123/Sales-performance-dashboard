### 1.  Project Title / Headline
# 📈 Regional Sales Performance Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive and dynamic Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across multiple cities, monitor target achievement, compare sales executives, and identify performance gaps for data-driven decision making.

## 2.  Overview

The Regional Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales activities across different cities including **Delhi, Chennai, Mumbai, Nagpur, Patna, Pune, Ranchi, and Surat**.

The dashboard enables managers and business leaders to monitor sales achievements, evaluate employee performance against targets, and identify areas requiring improvement through interactive visualizations and city-wise filtering.

## 3.  Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main platform used for dashboard creation and visualization.
- 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic filtering.
- 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Relationships among tables enable cross-filtering and interactive analysis.
- 📁 **File Formats**
  - `.pbix` for development
  - `.png` for dashboard preview

## 4.   Data Source

**Source:** Sample Sales Dataset containing regional sales information for multiple cities and sales executives.

### Dataset Includes:

- City/Region information
- Sales Executive details
- Total Sales achieved
- Target achievement percentage
- Target gap percentage
- Dashboard-wise performance metrics

The dataset is structured to support city-level and employee-level performance analysis.

# 5.  Features and Highlights

##   Business Problem

Sales managers often struggle to monitor regional sales performance and identify underperforming sales representatives in real time.

Key business questions include:

- Which city is generating the highest sales?
- Which sales executives are exceeding or missing their targets?
- How far are employees from achieving their assigned goals?
- Which region requires immediate managerial attention?
- How does performance vary across different locations?

Traditional spreadsheet reporting makes answering these questions time-consuming and inefficient.

## 6.   Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

- Provide a centralized view of sales performance across cities.
- Track sales achievements against targets.
- Enable city-wise performance comparisons.
- Identify high-performing and underperforming sales executives.
- Support data-driven sales strategy and decision-making.

## 7.   Dashboard Walkthrough

###   City Selection Panel

An interactive city slicer allows users to filter the entire dashboard by location:

- Chennai
- Delhi
- Mumbai
- Nagpur
- Patna
- Pune
- Ranchi
- Surat

This enables region-specific performance analysis.

###    Dashboard 1 — Sales Performance Table

Displays:

- Sales Executive Name
- Total Sales Generated

This visual provides a quick ranking of employees based on sales volume.

---

###   Dashboard 2 — Sales Comparison Analysis

Shows:

- Sales executives and their total sales values
- Comparative performance across team members

This helps managers identify leading contributors and performance variations.

---

###   Dashboard 3 — Target Achievement Analysis

A pie chart visualizes the **Target Hit Percentage** for each sales executive.

This helps answer:

- Who is achieving the highest percentage of their target?
- Which employees consistently perform well?
- What is the overall distribution of target achievement?

---

###   Dashboard 4 — Target Gap Analysis

A line chart displays the **Away From Target Percentage** for each sales executive.

This visual helps identify:

- Employees requiring additional support or coaching
- Performance gaps within the sales team
- Areas where intervention may improve results
